Jesus Christ Son of God and Saviour

Christians believe Jesus sacrificed Himself so that everyone who believes in him may live for ever. We can read about this in the bible. The four gospels (Matthew, Mark, Luke and John) tell the story of Jesus. And then book of Acts tells the story of the early church that grew in great numbers as Christians travelled from Jerusalem through Asia and Europe.
